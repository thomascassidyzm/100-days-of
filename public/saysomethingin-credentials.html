What We Do

The SSi Method was born from a simple insight:

Most language learning prepares you for "someday." But real life demands you speak NOW—in moments where failure is public and success is transformative.

We don't teach you to recognize words—we train you to speak with automatic confidence under pressure

  • Speaking your family's language at a wedding with relatives you've never met
  • Helping your children feel they belong when visiting family abroad

Professional Performers

  • Athletes integrating in foreign clubs (press conferences, locker room culture, fan engagement)
  • Public figures preparing for media appearances

Who Has Chosen Us

🎬 Iaith ar Daith — Preparing Celebrities for Public Performance

For 8 series of this award-winning show, we've prepared well-known public figures with little or no Welsh to perform entirely in Welsh on camera—usually within just days or weeks.

High Stakes Preparation:

  • Public performance with no room for failure—reputation on the line
  • Compressed timelines—often 4 to 7 days
  • Real audience judgment
Profound Transformations: Carol Vorderman reconnected with old friends • Ruth Jones made Welsh part of her daily life • Multiple celebrities became vocal advocates
🏛️ Welsh Government — National Centre Partnership

The National Centre for Learning Welsh was established in 2016 by the Welsh Government to oversee, coordinate, and improve all Welsh language learning for adults across Wales.

Its purpose supports Cymraeg 2050: A Million Welsh Speakers

SSi has been a trusted National Centre supplier for all their pupils since 2019

Why they chose us: Not because we're the largest Welsh language provider, but because we understand what's truly at stake—cultural preservation, national identity, and linguistic heritage that can never be replaced

Proof of Concept

🗾 Japanuary — 10 Days from Zero to Conversation

The "Japanuary" initiative demonstrates what's possible when you focus on performance, not perfection.

Three participants with no prior Japanese knowledge underwent our 10-day intensive and achieved:

Accelerated Confidence:

Ability to hold conversations—evidenced by 20-minute video call with native Japanese speakers

Real-World Communication:

Successfully ordered food in restaurants and engaged in everyday conversations with natural pronunciation and confident speech attempts

The proof: Real conversations, not scripted performance
🎓 Cardiff University Research — Secondary Schools
KEY FINDING: "Successful students with SSi are not necessarily the academically strong pupils"

Other Findings:

  • Positive impact on results for continuum content
  • Increased teacher engagement drove improved student results
  • Strong rates of language retention
  • Microlessons proved very popular—short, sharp bursts
  • Huge implications for improving education for all

Traditional education rewards students good at school.
SSi Method rewards students willing to speak—regardless of academic background.
